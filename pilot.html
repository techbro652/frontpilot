Is this actually free? What's the catch?
Yes, completely free for 30 days. No credit card, no contract, no obligation. We're a new agency and we want 2–3 businesses we can do great work for and point to as proof. In exchange for the free service, we ask for honest feedback and a testimonial if you're happy. That's the entire deal.
What happens after 30 days?
At the end of your trial, we'll have a short call to go over your results. If you want to continue, we'll talk through options. If not, no pressure — you keep whatever results we drove and walk away with no obligation. We don't auto-charge you anything.
How much time do I need to put in?
About 25 minutes total — a 15-minute onboarding call upfront and a 10-minute feedback call at the end. In between, we handle everything. You run your business, we handle your front desk.
Will the AI sound like us?
Yes. During your onboarding call, we learn your tone, vocabulary, and how you want your business represented. Everything we write and post is trained on your specific voice — not a generic template.
Why only 3 spots?
Because we're doing this right. We want to give each founding client real attention — not rush through 20 setups. Three clients means we can go deep, drive real results, and build case studies we're proud of.
