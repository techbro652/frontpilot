FAQ

Questions? We've got answers.

How does the AI sound? Will it sound robotic?
Not at all. During onboarding, we train the AI on your tone, vocabulary, and how you want to be represented. Review responses feel personal and genuine — not like a template. Most businesses can't tell the difference from a human-written reply.
Do I need any technical knowledge to set this up?
Zero. We handle everything — connecting your Google account, setting up the chat widget, configuring the SMS system. You fill out a simple form and we do the rest. Most clients are live within the same business day.
What if the AI gets something wrong?
You get a daily summary of everything Frontpilot did. You can review, edit, or delete any response. For anything sensitive (a complaint or legal matter), we flag it for your attention before responding.
How is this different from GoHighLevel or Birdeye?
Those are software platforms — you log in, configure them, and run them yourself. Frontpilot is a fully done-for-you service. You don't manage anything. We handle the setup, the daily operations, and the ongoing optimization. You just see the results.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, absolutely. No annual contracts, no cancellation fees. Month-to-month only. We earn your business every month.
