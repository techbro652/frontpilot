FAQ

Questions? We've got answers.

How does the AI sound? Will it sound robotic?
Not at all. During onboarding, we train the AI on your tone, vocabulary, and how you want to be represented. Review responses feel personal and genuine — not like a template. Most businesses can't tell the difference from a human-written reply.
How much does Frontari actually do? Do I have to manage anything?
Almost nothing. We handle setup, daily operations, and ongoing optimization. You fill out one onboarding form — that's it. You'll receive a monthly report showing exactly what we did, and you can flag anything you want us to adjust.
What if the AI gets something wrong?
You get a daily summary of everything we handled. You can review, edit, or delete any response. For anything sensitive — a complaint or a legal matter — we flag it for your attention before posting.
How is this different from GoHighLevel or Birdeye?
Those are software platforms — you log in, configure them, and run them yourself. Frontari is a fully done-for-you service. We handle the setup, the daily operations, and the ongoing optimization. You don't manage anything. You just see the results.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, absolutely. No annual contracts, no cancellation fees. Month-to-month only. We earn your business every month.
